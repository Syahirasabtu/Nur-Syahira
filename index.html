Hi fellas, i'm syahira and a Graphic Designer

Greetings, I'm Nur Syahira a dedicated graphic designer whose creative journey intertwines seamlessly with a profound passion for nurturing and cultivating indoor greenery.

Join me on this creative odyssey, where every project and plant serves as a testament to the power of design and the enduring allure of nature. Welcome to my world, where graphic design and houseplants converge to create a haven of inspiration and innovation.
